Giant mushrooms

Usually having a height between 1 and 2 meters, giant mushrooms are another type of "organism" able to withstand the harsh conditions of Mars.

Like all fungi, giant mushrooms of the Red planet are not plants and do not have the ability of photosynthesis. Instead they undergo chemosynthesis using hydrogen sulfide in Martian soil to support their growth.



Giant mushrooms have the following effects:

Food production-2
Lumber production+2

This type of vegetation can grow on following tile types:
  • Wasteland
  • Desert
  • Plains
