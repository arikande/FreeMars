Mutated cactus forest

Trees in a mutated cactus forest are not in fact cacti, the name comes from their somewhat resemblance to Saguaro Cacti of Arizona. Genetic engineers grew this species by splicing cactus DNA with some other plants.

In the initial stages terraforming Mars has a cold environment but it is also very dry. Plants growing on the arid surface of planet can conserve water thanks to their cacti DNA.



Mutated cactus forest have the following effects:

Food production-3
Lumber production+3

This type of vegetation can grow on following tile type:
  • Wasteland
  • Desert
  • Plains
