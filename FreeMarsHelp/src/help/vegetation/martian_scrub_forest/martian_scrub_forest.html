Martian scrub forest

A scrub forest is mainly composed of plants that are less than 8 metres (26 ft) high and usually have many stems arising at or near the base.

All of these plants are genetically engineered to resist Martian drought by such mechanisms as waxy, waterproof coatings on their leaves.



Martian scrub forest have the following effects:

Food production-4
Lumber production+3

This type of vegetation can grow on following tile type:
  • Desert
