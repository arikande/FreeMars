Auto manage water, food and fertilizer resources

This panel allows user to automanage colony's water, food and fertilizer resources. Auto managing water and food resources will assign required amount of colonists to water and food producing tiles or improvements from the "production workforce". If there is not enough colonists in "production workforce", it will not take any colonists from tiles extracting other resources like silica, or other resource manufacturing colony improvements like steel factory. It is advisable to keep a small percent of colonists in the producion workforce as a buffer.

When auto managing fertilizer is on, the colony will distribute its fertilizer resource to food producing tiles using 25 fertilizer per tile.
