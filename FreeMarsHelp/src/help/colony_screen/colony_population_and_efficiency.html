Colony population and efficiency

Colony's current population and efficiency is displayed here. The population of a colony will increase if it has enough food and water in its current stock; this is true even if it is not producing enough of these resources and its food or water stock is diminishing.

Following quantities of food and water is needed per colonist:

Food6
Water1
