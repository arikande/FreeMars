Food

Finding food to feed themselves has always been the toughest challenge for colonizers in human history and Mars is no exception. Even with terraforming (that is far from complete) barren wasteland of Red planet offers little opportunity for agriculture, especially in first years of colonization.

Martian settlers will have a hard time growing crops on Mars. Even genetically engineered plants suffer from harsh conditions of the planet.

The only terrain that is able to produce enough food to feed workers on it is plains, wastelands can also make a little addition to agriculture if supported with irrigation built on them.
