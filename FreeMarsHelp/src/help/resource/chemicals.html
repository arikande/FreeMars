Chemicals

Mars has many useful minerals like sulfates or chlorides, but sending these raw resources to Earth will yield little income. Credits coming from minerals can support an early economy but as the population and number of improvements to support it increases, new income sources are needed.

Produced at the chemical plants using minerals and energy, chemicals will give more income to colonies than minerals. As the production and maintainance costs are high for chemical plants, it is feasible to build these improvements only in larger colonies.
