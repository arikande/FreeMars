Martian resources

Harvesting and mining Martian resources was the driving force behind terraforming and then colonizing of the planet. With strict regulations on Earth's resources, colonies on Red planet are to be new sources of goods for mankind.

Following types of resources exist on Red planet:
  • Food
  • Energy
  • Hydrogen
  • Lumber
  • Iron
  • Steel
  • Silica
  • Glass
  • Minerals
  • Chemicals
