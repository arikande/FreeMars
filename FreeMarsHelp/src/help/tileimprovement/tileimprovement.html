Terrain improvements

Inhospitable terrain of Mars would be of little use to colonizers if it was not improved with roads, irrigation or mines.

Of these improvements, irrigation is most crucial for the survival of Martian population since producing enough food would be impossible without it.

Roads would allow rapid transportation of goods and resources between cities.

Mines would increase iron and mineral output of cities thus increasing income for an early economy.

Following improvements can be built on Martian terrain:
  • Irrigation
  • Mines
  • Roads
