Roads

Roads are essential for rapid transportation of extracted resources and produced goods between colonies. Since the income from tax is very little, exports to Earth are the fuel of Martian economy.

The shuttles carrying these exports can land on and take off from colonies having a starport, which is an expensive facility to build in every colony. This requires goods to be transported by land from the colonies not having this improvement.

A good network of roads speeds up this transportation process considerably, allowing a stronger economy.

Roads also cause a slight increase in tax income.
