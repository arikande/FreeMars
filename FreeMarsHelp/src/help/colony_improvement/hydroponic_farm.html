Hydroponic farm

Hydroponic farms are colony facilities in which plants are grown using mineral nutrient solutions, in water, without soil. Normally, soil itself is not required for plant growth, it only acts as a mineral nutrient container for plant roots to absorb them. It is possible for a plant to thrive only in water as long as the necessary mineral nutrients exist in the water supply.

Even after terraforming and introduction of liquid water, Martian soil is not much suitable for growing plants because of its chemical structure. Building improvements like irrigation on this terrain need long periods of time and expensive work. Because of this, hydroponic farms are an important addition to colony's food supply.
