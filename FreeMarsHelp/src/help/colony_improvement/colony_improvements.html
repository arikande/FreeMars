Colony improvements

Many types of improvements may be built in Martian colonies to provide production, economical or defensive advantages.

While some of the improvements are essential for colony's survival others are just used for storing goods.

Following improvements can be built on Martian colonies:
  • Arena
  • Bank
  • Chemical plant
  • Defense turrets
  • Factory
  • Food silo
  • Glass factory
  • Granary
  • Hydrogen collector
  • Hydroponic farm
  • Market
  • Mars transit system
  • Mech barracks
  • Solar array
  • Starport
  • Starshipyard
  • Steel factory
  • Theater
  • Trading post
  • Wall
  • Workshop
