Transporter

The Transporter is a simple unit used to carry goods between Martian colonies.

This unit does not have any armor or armament and can not fight, it will be destroyed and its cargo lost even if an enemy unit with minimum arms attacks it, thus the transporter must not be left undefended.

This unit can carry 1000 tons of following goods:
  • Food
  • Stone
  • Iron
  • Lumber
  • Silica


Movement points 2
Attack 0
Defence 0
Production cost 2000
Upkeep cost 60

Can move on:
  • Plains
  • Desert
  • Wasteland
  • Tundra
