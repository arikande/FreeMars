Militia

A militia unit can be formed by recruiting 100 colonists at a militia barracks. Armed with gauss rifles but having little military training, this unit is more suitable for defense than offense and can be used as garrison forces in colonies.


Movement points 1
Attack 10
Defense 20
Production cost 4000
Upkeep cost 40
Exploration radius 1

Can move on:
  • Plains
  • Desert
  • Wasteland
  • Tundra
  • Crater
  • Hills
