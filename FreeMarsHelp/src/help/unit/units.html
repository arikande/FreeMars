Units

Units that are used for Martian exploration and colonization need special design and production since they will endure a very different environment from Earth.

These vehicles and shuttles must continue to work properly in a hostile and cold terrain with a low gravity of 0.376 g.

Following units are used on Mars:
  • Colonizer
  • Engineer
  • Scout
  • Shuttle
  • Militia
  • Mech
  • Transporter
