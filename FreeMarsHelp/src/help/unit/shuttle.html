Shuttle

The SC-1907 shuttle is the pack mule of Martian colonization, it is used to transport all kinds of materials between Mars and Earth. These goods form the backbone of an early Martian economy, as credits from exported iron and silica are the only notable source of income in first years of colonization.

This spaceship will need 5 turns to travel between Mars and Earth, and its cargo bays can carry 3000 tons of following goods:
  • Food
  • Stone
  • Iron
  • Lumber
  • Silica
