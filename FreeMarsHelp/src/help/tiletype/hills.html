Hills

Hills are more common on the southern hemisphere of Mars, whereas the northern hemisphere is generally flat. There is very little tectonic activity on planet, because of this Martian hills are old and generally smooth.

While causing some problems for transportation, this type of terrain has good iron, silica and mineral production, but it will produce very little food if used for agriculture. In battle, it will provide significant advantages to defender.
