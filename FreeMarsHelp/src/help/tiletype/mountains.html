Mountains

While mountains are rare on Mars, the planet is also the site of Olympus Mons, the highest known mountain within the Solar System

Movement in this type of terrain is difficult, but mountains have good iron, magnesium and aluminium production, it will not produce any food though. In battle, it will provide significant advantages to defender, doubling its strength.
