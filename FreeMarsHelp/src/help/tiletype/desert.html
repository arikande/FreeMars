Desert

Martian deserts are not hot like Earth deserts, but they resemble in amount of precipitation they receive. Rain is very rare in poorly terraformed Mars and it is almost non existent in desert terrain of the Red planet.

This type of terrain has some silica deposits and very little food can be produced. In battle, it will not provide any advantages to defender.

Improvements:
  • Irrigation
  • Roads
